About Me

I am currently a senior Computer Science Major at Stonehill College.

I am interested in cybersecurity but have a strong desire to learn as much as I can. Learning has always been apart of my life due to a genuine curiosty of how things work.

Being from Maine, I've always enjoyed the outdoors, hiking trails, biking and wanting to understand the nature around me. That same curiosity led me to computer science and a desire to understand the technology that surrounds me.

I have been a three sport athlete my whole life. Football, basketball and lacrosse were my 3 sports of choice. I then went on to be captain for football in highschool and be a leader on a championship team. Then in college I was unrecruited and got an offer to play football as a preferred walk on. This was an opportunity and I made the most of it. I worked my way onto the field freshman year and then to a rotational player sophmore year and then starting and voted captain as a junior. All this while maintaining a high GPA and being on the Dean's list every semester was a challenge.

All of this while balancing D1 football at Stonehill, was my piece in proving how you do anything is how you do everything.
